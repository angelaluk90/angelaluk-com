A BIT ABOUT ME

UX/UI Designer with a background in Industrial and Product Design. Technically minded with a creative and lateral approach to problem solving.

Whether it's a physical product, an interface or both, I'm excited by the challenge of understanding how a product will be used and the problem it aims to solve. I strive to create products that are fluid and seamless to use as well as creating beautiful interfaces and form.

Away from designing products, I skydive, play netball and can solve a Rubik's cube in 90 seconds.
